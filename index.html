About / Purpose

Gangs Wars: FPS Pixel Shooter is a mobile sandbox action game. The purpose of this app is to entertain users by providing an open-world city with missions, physics-based driving, FPS combat, mini-games, and optional online play. This app is for entertainment only and does not offer financial, transactional, or sensitive services.

Key Functionality

  • Explore a large open-world pixel city with physics-based gameplay.
  • Story missions, challenges, police chases, and ragdoll mechanics.
  • Drive cars, bikes, helicopters; discover secrets and mini-games.
  • Play offline or join online events and multiplayer battles.
  • Cloud save and achievements via Google services (optional).

How It Works

  1. Install the app from Google Play or the App Store.
  2. Optionally sign in with your Google account to sync progress and achievements.
  3. Play missions, free-roam, or compete in online events.

What Data We Access & Why

Google Sign-In (optional)

  • openid, email, profile — to create your in-game account, show your name/avatar, and let you restore access.

Google Play Games Services (optional)

  • Achievements / leaderboards — to record your progress and rankings.
  • Cloud save — to sync gameplay across devices.

We do not request or process sensitive scopes (contacts, Drive files, payments, etc.). We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for advertising.

Game Overview

Gangs Wars is a physics-driven sandbox shooter in a block-city setting with theft missions, mafia storylines, high-speed police chases, and ragdoll fun. Plan your own route: race, fight, discover secrets, and play at your own pace.
